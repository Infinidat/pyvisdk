
==================================================================================================
EdgeStatus
==================================================================================================

.. describe:: EdgeStatus

    Values to convey information about the status of an edge in the topology. up - indicates a fully operational state. degraded - indicates potential non-critical problems. down - indicates a critical failure. unknown - indicates that the status is not known. notReported - indicates that the status is not reported.
